About Us

Nested in Museyo next to badagry, just one hour by road from Cotonou. The boon Resort is the first of it's kind, award winning, all-inclusive, family run, oceanfront gateway in Lagos

Three full meals (and snacks) await our overnight guests; a fusion of international favourites and locally sourced ingridients - from the land and the sea. Lunchtime is where your indulgent visit begins! Dinner alternates between a three-course menu and a BBQ feast!

In any one of the nine beautifully-finished en-suite bedrooms; five standard queen bed, and four family width queen (room #6) or king bed (rooms #7-9) with a 2 x 4 foot mattress bunk bed, comfort is guaranteed with all amenities provided to ensure you sleep peacefully, waking only to the sound waves from the Atlantic Ocean

You are highly welcome and we surely hope to see you soon!
